UXR - Kode

Problem Statement

Digital learning has become a trend. And there are millions of digital learners in India who are learning coding languages. I am interested in creating a digital product for college students who study coding languages in their leisure time to gain new skills. Since college students usually have a busy schedule, our platform aims at optimizing study plans for college students.

Description/ Idea behind it/ solution

Covid has a great impact on society and everyone is learning online, meanwhile, there are a lot of platforms who provide online learning but failed to solve student problems, Many students can't learn online because of data connectivity, low-end devices, cost of the courses. They failed to understand the user's needs. Many platforms don't even consider accessibility.

The idea behind it is to meet the user's needs and provide the best options for all the users and consider all representatives who are missing out.

The solution is to provide many options like - live lectures, offline lectures, all main accessibility features, one on one guidance, 24/7 support, solution videos of assignment and questions, discounts, scholarship, contest, etc.

Name of your App

Kode

Define the target audience

Students with a full-time college who:

  • Use digital coding language-learning products at least once a week
  • Have been using digital coding language-learning products for at least 3 months

User personas / User needs and goals

Client goals

The Client's goal is to get users to learn new skills and to stay productive, remain consistent and focused during the day so the user can't waste their time watching reels.
